Scalar Wiring Guide (Non-Inductive)

To create a true scalar field (zero-point magnetics), the current must flow in opposite directions through the parallel wires to cancel the magnetic field.

1. Take the two ends of the speaker wire at the OUTSIDE of the coil.

2. Strip them and twist/solder them together (creates a U-turn).

3. Hook your amplifier/PWM +/- to the two bare wires at the INSIDE of the core.
