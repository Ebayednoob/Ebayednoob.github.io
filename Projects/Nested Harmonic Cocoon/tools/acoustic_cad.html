Hybrid System Tips

  • Subwoofers (174Hz / 285Hz): Place on the floor. Low frequencies are omnidirectional. Focus on constructive phase match (>90%).
  • Micro-Arrays (High Freq): Use the "Point at Target" feature. Place in a halo around the head for spatial sweeping.
  • Phase Match: Green (100%) means the wave crest hits the listener perfectly. Red (0%) creates a dead zone (cancellation).
