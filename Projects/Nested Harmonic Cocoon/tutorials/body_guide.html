Why Tempered Glass?

The foundation of the cocoon is a 44.25" x 22.00" sheet of half-inch tempered glass. Glass is utilized instead of wood or metal for three critical engineering reasons:

  • Dielectric Permittivity: Glass is an electrical insulator but allows magnetic scalar fields to pass through completely unimpeded without causing eddy currents (which would occur in a metal frame).
  • Optical Transparency: Allows the 3W LEDs mounted underneath the bed to shine their collated photons directly upward through the crystal waveguides and into the subject.
  • Acoustic Density: Glass serves as a highly rigid boundary layer. When acoustic DML standing waves hit the glass, it creates a rigid reflection point, allowing us to map cymatic nodes accurately.
