# The 7 Chakras: Frequencies, Light, and Crystals

In modern sound healing, color therapy (chromotherapy), and crystal healing, different vibrational tools are mapped to the traditional 7-chakra system. By combining Solfeggio frequencies, specific light wavelengths, and corresponding crystals, you can create a deeply synergistic environment for meditation and energetic balance.

## Detailed Breakdown by Chakra

### 1. Root Chakra (Muladhara)

- **Frequency:** 396 Hz — *Liberating Guilt and Fear.* Used to turn grief into joy, release hidden blockages, and help you feel grounded and secure.
- **Light / Color:** Red. In color therapy, red light stimulates physical energy, vitality, and grounding connection to the earth.
- **Crystals:** Red Jasper (for stamina), Black Tourmaline (for deep protection and clearing negative energy), Hematite (for grounding).

---

### 2. Sacral Chakra (Svadhisthana)

- **Frequency:** 417 Hz — *Undoing Situations and Facilitating Change.* Clears traumatic experiences, promotes moving forward, and enhances creativity.
- **Light / Color:** Orange. Orange light is warm and stimulating, used to boost creativity, emotional expression, and joy.
- **Crystals:** Carnelian (for courage and creative fire), Orange Calcite (for emotional balancing), Sunstone (for warmth and abundance).

---

### 3. Solar Plexus Chakra (Manipura)

- **Frequency:** 528 Hz — *Transformation and Miracles (DNA Repair).* The "Miracle Tone," associated with increased energy, personal will, and self-belief.
- **Light / Color:** Yellow. Yellow light therapy is uplifting and associated with mental clarity, digestion (metaphorical and physical), and confidence.
- **Crystals:** Citrine (the "merchant's stone" for manifestation and willpower), Pyrite (for confidence), Yellow Jasper (for focused energy).

---

### 4. Heart Chakra (Anahata)

- **Frequency:** 639 Hz — *Connecting and Relationships.* Enhances communication, tolerance, and love. Used to repair relationships and build deep connections.
- **Light / Color:** Green (and Pink). Green light is deeply balancing and restorative for the heart. Pink represents gentle, unconditional love.
- **Crystals:** Rose Quartz (the ultimate stone of unconditional love), Green Aventurine (for heart-healing and luck), Jade (for harmony).

---

### 5. Throat Chakra (Vishuddha)

- **Frequency:** 741 Hz — *Expression and Solutions.* Empowers you to speak your truth authentically and acts as a mental cleanse to solve problems.
- **Light / Color:** Light Blue / Cyan. Blue light has a cooling, calming effect that encourages truth, vocal clarity, and open communication.
- **Crystals:** Lapis Lazuli (for deep truth and self-awareness), Aquamarine (for calming the voice), Sodalite (for rational thought and verbalization).

---

### 6. Third Eye Chakra (Ajna)

- **Frequency:** 852 Hz — *Returning to Spiritual Order.* Awakens inner strength, clears mental blockages, and opens deeper intuition.
- **Light / Color:** Indigo. Indigo light is used to stimulate the pineal gland, encourage deep sleep, and open the mind to spiritual awareness.
- **Crystals:** Amethyst (for spiritual awakening and psychic protection), Fluorite (for mental clarity and focus), Labradorite (for mystical insight).

---

### 7. Crown Chakra (Sahasrara)

- **Frequency:** 963 Hz — *Divine Consciousness or Awakening.* The "Frequency of the Gods," connecting you with the highest consciousness and pure light.
- **Light / Color:** Violet or White (Full Spectrum). Violet is the highest frequency visible light, representing spiritual connection. White light encompasses all colors, representing pure divine consciousness.
- **Crystals:** Clear Quartz (the master healer and amplifier), Selenite (for pure divine light and space clearing), Moonstone (for feminine/divine connection).

---

> **Note:** The original Solfeggio scale consisted of 6 tones (396, 417, 528, 639, 741, 852). As the system was integrated into chakra healing, 963 Hz was widely adopted to complete the traditional 7-chakra system.
