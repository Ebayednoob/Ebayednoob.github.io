3. App Interface & User Experience

The companion app (iOS/Android) acts as the bridge between the visual, auditory, and physical experiences.

Dual-Audio Routing: The app intelligently routes standard audio (voice/music) to the user's wireless headphones or smart TV, while sending a separate, isolated low-frequency (LFE) track directly to the pad via Bluetooth 5.0.

Visualizer Mode: If casting to a TV or VR headset, the app displays a stunning, generative visualizer that reacts to the current chakra phase (e.g., swirling red fractals during the Root phase, shifting to orange water ripples for the Sacral).

Intensity Sliders: Users can customize their experience via the app, adjusting the intensity of the vibration and the brightness of the LEDs to suit sensory sensitivities.

Custom Sessions: Beyond the 1-hour master alignment, the app offers targeted 15-minute sessions (e.g., "Deep Sleep Grounding" using only Root/Sacral, or "Creative Flow" using Sacral/Throat).

4. Safety & Contraindications

Because this device utilizes powerful low-frequency tactile sound and bright localized light, the following safety guidelines must be included in the user manual:

Pregnancy: Consult a physician before using vibroacoustic therapy, especially the abdominal zones.

Pacemakers & Implants: The transducers utilize strong magnets; users with pacemakers or sensitive electronic implants should avoid use.

Epilepsy/Photosensitivity: Chromotherapy lights should be capable of being disabled for users sensitive to changing or pulsing lights.

Psychological Safety: Deep meditative states induced by NMDA-assisted therapies or simple deep trance can cause sudden emotional releases. Users are encouraged to have a safe, quiet space and integration tools (like journaling) ready post-session.
